# Pricing

Three-tier SaaS on top of a free, open SDK.

| Tier | Customer | Price | What you get |
|------|----------|-------|--------------|
| **OSS SDK** | Anyone | Free, Apache 2.0 (publication deferred while repo is private) | Capture, distill, store, retrieve, receipts, all in-process |
| **SMB Gainshare** | < $250K/yr reasoning spend | 15-20% of measured saved-token cost | Hosted server, signed receipts, EU-AI-Act compliance pack |
| **Mid-Market Flat** | $250K – $5M/yr | $2K – $10K / month | Above + SLA + custom embedder + dedicated compliance officer |
| **Enterprise** | > $5M/yr | $50K – $150K / year | Above + on-prem option + per-model conditional-conformal calibration |
| **Audit-trail add-on** | Compliance-regulated | $30K – $50K / year | Notified-body-ready evidence binders + quarterly attestation |

## Comparable price bands (sub-agent research, May 2026)

- Helicone: free OSS / $20-seat / custom enterprise. 20-30% typical savings.
- Portkey: $49 base → $2-10K/mo enterprise. $9 / 100K logs.
- Credo AI (compliance peer): $30-150K/yr enterprise.
- Trustible / Holistic / Fairly: similar band.

## Math on the SMB tier

Assume a customer paying $30K/month for reasoning compute at o3-pro
($20/$80 per MTok). If Anamnesis saves 40% of reasoning tokens, monthly
savings are $12K. At 15% gainshare, our revenue is $1.8K/mo per such
customer — fully on-savings, zero customer risk.

Ten such customers = $18K/mo MRR purely on the smallest tier. Two
mid-market accounts at the top of band ($10K/mo each) double that.

## What we do NOT charge for

- The SDK itself, ever.
- Calibration data submitted by the customer.
- Compliance documentation pulls.

## What we DO charge for, transparently

- Hosted storage + retrieval per accepted reuse.
- Signed-receipt issuance per accepted reuse.
- Notified-body evidence pack assembly (quarterly).
